Cricket is more than just a game,

A field of passion, a world of fame.

With bat in hand and dreams held high,

Players chase glory beneath the sky.

The bowler runs with steady pace,

Determined look upon the face.

The batter waits with fearless sight,

Ready to turn the ball to flight.

Cheers erupt from every stand,

Flags are waving across the land.

Every boundary, every six,

Fills the hearts with joyful tricks.

A diving catch, a spinning ball,

Moments remembered by one and all.

Victory smiles, defeat may sting,

Yet hope returns with every spring.

From dusty streets to stadium lights,

Cricket inspires countless fights.

It teaches courage, skill, and grace,

To rise again in every race.

So let the willow sing once more,

As crowds unite with a mighty roar.

For cricket lives in every heart,

A timeless game, a work of art.
